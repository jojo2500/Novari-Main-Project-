Frequently Asked Questions

Vintage sizing can differ from modern sizing standards. We provide detailed measurements for each item, including bust, waist, hips, and length. We recommend comparing these measurements to a similar piece in your wardrobe that fits you well. Our size guide offers additional information on how to measure yourself accurately.

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be in original condition with all tags attached. To initiate a return, please contact us at returns@novari.com with your order number. Please note that shipping costs are non-refundable. Visit our Shipping & Returns page for more details.

Our team of vintage experts sources items from around the world, including estate sales, vintage markets, and trusted collectors. Each piece is carefully selected for its quality, condition, and unique character. We authenticate all items and provide detailed information about their era and history when available.
