The Novari Journey

Preserving the past, curating the present, and inspiring the future of fashion.

Our Beginning

Novari was born in 2015 from a passion for history, craftsmanship, and sustainable fashion. Founder Emma Novak started with a small collection of vintage pieces she had gathered during her travels as a fashion journalist. What began as a pop-up shop in Brooklyn quickly grew into a beloved destination for vintage enthusiasts and fashion-forward individuals alike.

The name "Novari" combines Emma's surname with "ari," meaning "treasure" in several languages—perfectly capturing our mission to uncover and preserve fashion treasures from the past.

Today, our team of dedicated vintage experts travels the world to source unique, high-quality pieces that tell a story and stand the test of time. Each item in our collection is carefully selected, authenticated, and restored when necessary to ensure it can continue its journey with a new owner.

Our Philosophy

At Novari, we believe that fashion should be timeless, sustainable, and personal. Our philosophy is built on three core principles:

Quality Over Quantity

We focus on exceptional craftsmanship and materials that have proven their durability over decades. Each piece in our collection is chosen for its quality, condition, and unique character.

Sustainability Through History

By giving vintage clothing a second life, we're reducing waste and the environmental impact of fashion. Choosing vintage is one of the most sustainable ways to express your personal style.

Stories Worth Wearing

Every vintage piece has a history—a story woven into its fabric. We research the provenance, era, and cultural context of our items, allowing you to connect with the rich narrative behind what you wear.

Our Values

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing fashion's environmental impact by extending the lifecycle of quality garments and promoting conscious consumption.

Craftsmanship

We honor the skilled artisans of the past by preserving their work and educating our community about traditional techniques and quality construction.

Community

We foster a community of vintage enthusiasts, sharing knowledge and passion for fashion history while creating meaningful connections around shared values.
